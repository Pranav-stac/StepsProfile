About STEPS - Somaiya Technology for Efficient Plot Surveying

STEPS is an indigenous app developed by the student-faculty team within the Somaiya ecosystem to digitize farm boundaries for sugarcane farmers associated with Godavari Biorefineries Ltd. This app is a part of the Centre for Achieving Sustainable Climate Action, Development, and Engagement (CASCADE)'s foundational project aimed at building a comprehensive database and revolutionizing the way farm data is collected and managed, ensuring precision and efficiency in agricultural practices.
