Privacy Policy

Introduction

STEPS (Somaiya Technology for Efficient Plot Surveying) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Only authorized users as approved by the GBL can use this app. We collect information directly from you when you use our app, including:

  • Personal Information: Farmer's name, contact details, and any other farm information provided by farmers and farm supervisors.
  • Farm Data: Location, boundaries, and other relevant agricultural information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Digitize and manage farm boundaries.
  • Improve our services and app functionality.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share your data with:

  • Authorized members of the Somaiya ecosystem for project-related purposes.
  • Service providers who assist us in operating the app and delivering our services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
